Specialized Care from Board-Certified Psychiatric Experts

At MyndWorks Psychiatry, we understand that mental health challenges like anxiety and depression are not just “chemical imbalances” — they are complex conditions influenced by biology, trauma, stress, relationships, and life circumstances. That’s why our board-certified psychiatric providers take a comprehensive, individualized approach that treats the whole person, not just the diagnosis.

Unlike traditional clinics where appointments are rushed and patients feel like just another chart number, we dedicate the time needed to truly understand your unique situation. Our longer appointment times (initial evaluations up to 90 minutes, follow-ups 25–50 minutes) allow us to build trust, adjust treatment thoughtfully, and celebrate your progress every step of the way.

Anxiety Disorders

Generalized Anxiety, Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety, Phobias, and Performance Anxiety — treated with therapy integration, targeted medications, and advanced options like beta-blockers or short-term benzodiazepine alternatives when appropriate.

Major Depressive Disorder

Including treatment-resistant depression. We offer genetic testing, augmentation strategies, Spravato (esketamine), ketamine-assisted treatment referrals, and TMS coordination.

ADHD in Adults & Adolescents

Comprehensive evaluation with rating scales, executive function assessment, and personalized stimulant/non-stimulant regimens tailored to your lifestyle and goals.
